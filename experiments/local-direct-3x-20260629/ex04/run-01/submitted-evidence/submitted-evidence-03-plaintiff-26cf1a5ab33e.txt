Source: UNDOF Mandate page, https://undof.unmissions.org/en/undof-mandate.
Captured relevant text from indexed page:
"The United Nations Disengagement Observer Force (UNDOF) was established by Security Council resolution 350 (1974) of 31 May 1974 to: Maintain the ceasefire between Israel and Syria; Supervise the Disengagement of Israeli and Syrian Forces Agreement and its Protocol with regard to the areas of separation and limitation."

The same page's indexed text states: "UNDOF helps to supervise disengagement of Israeli and Syrian forces, as per the 1974 Disengagement Agreement. It maintains the ceasefire and monitors and observes the area of separation and the ceasefire line between Israel and Syria."

Corroborating UN Peacekeeping factsheet snippet, https://peacekeeping.un.org/en/factsheet/undof:
"Since then, UNDOF has remained in the area to maintain the ceasefire between the Israeli and Syrian forces and to supervise the implementation of the disengagement agreement."

Corroborating UN Peacemaker page, https://peacemaker.un.org/en/node/9424:
"This agreement seeks to implement United Nations Security Council Resolution 338 dated 22 October 1973. The agreement establishes a zone of separation, calls for the disengagement of forces and the release of prisoners of war. It also authorises the United Nations Disengagement Observer Force (UNDOF) to supervise the agreement."
