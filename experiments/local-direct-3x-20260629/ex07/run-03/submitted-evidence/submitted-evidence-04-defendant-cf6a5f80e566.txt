Extracted facts from visible page text:
- The decree is dated May 8, 2026 and signed by President of Ukraine V. Zelenskyy.
- The decree concerns conducting a parade in Moscow on May 9, 2026.
- It states that, taking into account numerous requests and for a humanitarian purpose outlined in negotiations with the American side on May 8, 2026, the president decrees permission to hold the May 9 parade in Moscow, Russian Federation.
- For the duration of the parade, from 10:00 Kyiv time on May 9, 2026, the territorial square of Red Square is excluded from Ukrainian weapons targeting plans.
- The page lists four coordinate points for the Red Square area.
- The decree takes effect from the date of signing.
