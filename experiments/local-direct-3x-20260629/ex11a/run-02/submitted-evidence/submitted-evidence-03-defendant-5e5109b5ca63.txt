Visible title/summary: "All west Tehran FIR airports are closed except listed airports operating sunrise to sunset, with new CAA permission required for civil IFR passenger flights."
Period of applicability shown: UTC 2026-05-22 20:46 - 2026-05-25 08:30. Category shown: AD Airport Operations.
Interpretation shown: All airports in the west part of Tehran FIR are closed except OITR, OIKK, OIAA, OISS, OIYY, OICC, OIGG, and OIAW, which are operational from sunrise to sunset. All previous permissions are suspended for all operators, and new permission must be requested from the CAA for operation of civil IFR passenger flights on these airports.
