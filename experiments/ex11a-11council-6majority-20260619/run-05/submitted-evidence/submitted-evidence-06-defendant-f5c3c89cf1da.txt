Title: Appendix B. International NOTAM (Q) Codes
Publisher/source: Federal Aviation Administration, FAA Order 7930.2
Retrieved: 2026-06-20T00:51:00Z
URL: https://www.faa.gov/air_traffic/publications/atpubs/notam_html/appendix_b.html

Relevant captured text:
- This appendix is used to interpret coded international NOTAMs. A NOTAM code group contains five letters.
- The first letter is always Q to indicate a NOTAM code abbreviation.
- The second and third letters identify the subject being reported.
- The fourth and fifth letters identify the status of operation of the subject being reported.

Relevant subject-code entries:
- Under ATM Airspace Organization: AF = Flight information region (uniform phraseology: fir).
- Under ATM Airspace Organization: AR = ATS route (uniform phraseology: ats route).
- Under AGA Facilities and Services: FA = Aerodrome (uniform phraseology: ad).

Relevant status-code entries:
- Under Limitations: LC = Closed (uniform phraseology: clsd).
- Under Limitations: LI = Closed to IFR operations.
- Under Limitations: LV = Closed to VFR operations.
