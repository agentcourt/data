Administration of Donald J. Trump, 2025

Title: Remarks in an Exchange With Reporters Prior to the 2026 Fédération Internationale des Associations de Football (FIFA) World Cup Draw
Date: December 5, 2025

Opening note: [The President's remarks were joined in progress.]

Excerpt - The President's Interest in Soccer (web.open lines 196-203):
Q. Should there be a special final: American—[inaudible]?
The President. No, but I got to meet Ronaldo the other day, came to the White House. He was fantastic. He's a handsome guy and a great guy. And my son Barron and him—and they had a big conversation. And my son loves soccer—"football," as you would say. But Ronaldo is great. He's a great guy. He's got the whole package. He's a great player.
Thank you very much. Thank you. [Inaudible]

Excerpt - The President's Interest in Soccer (web.open lines 322-329):
Q. Did you ever play soccer—or football?
The President. Believe it or not, I did. I did, and I loved it. I had a—I was okay. I wouldn't say—I was not Ronaldo or Messi. I was a little bit short of them. [Laughter] But——
Mr. Infantino. Just a little bit.
The President. But they have a little more speed. But no, I was fine. But I loved playing soccer.

Timing/context note (web.open lines 461-462):
NOTE: The President spoke at 11:13 a.m. at the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts. In his remarks, he referred to Trey Yingst, chief foreign correspondent, Fox News Channel; Cristiano Ronaldo, forward for Saudi Pro League club Al-Nass and Portugal's national soccer team; and Lionel Messi, forward for Major League Soccer club Inter Miami and Argentina's national team. A reporter referred to New York State Attorney General Letitia James.
