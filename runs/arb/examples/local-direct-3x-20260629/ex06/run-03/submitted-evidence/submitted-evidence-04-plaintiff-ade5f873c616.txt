Title: Fact Sheet: President Donald J. Trump Safeguards Venezuelan Oil Revenue for the Good of the American and Venezuelan People
Source: The White House
URL: https://www.whitehouse.gov/fact-sheets/2026/01/fact-sheet-president-donald-j-trump-safeguards-venezuelan-oil-revenue-for-the-good-of-the-american-and-venezuelan-people/
Date shown on page: January 9, 2026
Retrieval: web.open 2026-06-30

Relevant extracted page text from search/open result:

"PROTECTING VENEZUELAN OIL REVENUE: Today, President Donald J. Trump signed an Executive Order declaring a national emergency to safeguard Venezuelan oil revenue held in U.S. Treasury accounts from attachment or judicial process, ensuring these funds are preserved to advance U.S. foreign policy objectives."

"Last week, U.S. armed forces conducted an extraordinary military operation in support of law enforcement to bring outlaw dictator Maduro to justice."

"It is in the national interest of the United States to be surrounded by safe, secure, and stable countries; The President's actions reestablish deterrence in the Western hemisphere."

This source is submitted as an official White House confirmation of the operation and as limiting context: the White House characterized the operation as in support of law enforcement, not expressly as territorial occupation.
