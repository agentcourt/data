Subject line: Imposing Maximum Pressure on the Government of the Islamic Republic of Iran, Denying Iran All Paths to a Nuclear Weapon, and Countering Iran's Malign Influence.

Relevant extracted text:
- The memorandum states that the Government of Iran has declared hostility to the United States and its allies and partners.
- It describes Iran as the world's leading state sponsor of terror and the IRGC as a designated Foreign Terrorist Organization.
- It says Iran's behavior threatens the national interest of the United States.
- It states that it is in the national interest to impose maximum pressure on the Iranian regime to end its nuclear threat, curtail its ballistic missile program, and stop support for terrorist groups.
- Section 1, Policy: the United States policy is that Iran be denied a nuclear weapon and intercontinental ballistic missiles; that Iran's network and campaign of regional aggression be neutralized; that the IRGC and its surrogates be disrupted, degraded, or denied access to resources; and to counter Iran's development of missiles and asymmetric and conventional weapons capabilities.
